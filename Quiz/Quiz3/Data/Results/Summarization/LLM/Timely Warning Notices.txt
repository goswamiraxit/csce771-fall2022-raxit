 The University of South Carolina makes timely warnings to the University Community regarding certain crimes considered to be a serious or continuing threat to students, faculty, staff, and visitors . Such warnings will not compromise an on-going criminal investigation . The Division of Law Enforcement and Safety (DLES) may decide that a warning must be issued if a crime has the potential to compromise the safety of the University . The University may send timely warnings by various communication media including: University e-mail, Carolina Alert social media; Carolina Alert website; and Carolina Alert RSS feed; and the DLES web site on the crime alert page: http://bit.ly/1JGV46m. The DLES Associate Vice President-Chief of Police or his/her designee will be responsible for authorizing and issuing timely warnings, on behalf of the university .
